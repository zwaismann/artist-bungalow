Vehicles

PARKING

The driveway fits one car comfortably. There are also three street spots on the cul-de-sac directly in front of the house. The street is quiet and parking is never an issue. If you're arriving without a car, rideshares work great from this location.

Important

A FEW THINGS TO KNOW

NO SMOKING

No smoking inside the home. The deck and outdoor areas are fine.

NO PARTIES

No parties or events. We love a good time, but the neighbors do too.

QUIET HOURS

Quiet hours after 10 PM. The cul-de-sac is peaceful, let's keep it that way.

PET POLICY

Pets are welcome. Just let us know in advance and keep them off the furniture.

ART & DECOR

Please don't move the art or instruments. They're in their happy places.

LOCK UP

Lock up when you leave. The neighborhood is safe, but it's still LA.

The Vibe

HOWARD THE VINTAGE PIANO

Meet Howard, our vintage upright piano. He isn't just decoration—you are more than welcome to play him. We only ask that you play him gently and lovingly, as he is an older, delicate instrument. Put on a record, flip through the books, and make yourself at home.
